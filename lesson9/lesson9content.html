Finding scale of the song

When you listen a song,you will interested to play that song by listen without seeing any song notation.TO play a song without seeing song notation firstly you have to listen song carefully and find the song notes by press each note in the keyboard.It is very difficult to find the tune or song by pressing and seeing all notes in the keyboard.But it become very easy by finding the scale of the song or any tune.

Note: You have to know all the scales then you have to know how to find the scale.
How to find a scale of the song?

To find the scale of a song we have two methods. These two methods are very helpful to you to find the scale of the song.

  • Method-1
    • Firstly we have listen the song which you have to play your own until that song comes to your mind.
    • Now we see the last of word in the pallavi and sing that last letter of pallavi continuously.
    • After we have to find that last letter in the keyboard with by singing.Compulsory one note in keyboard was matched with that last letter.
    • Now we have play a major and minor with that note(make this note as root note in chord).
    • Now observe that two major and minor chords which one matched to the song.Compulsory one chord was matched to the song either major chord or minor chord.
    • So you can find the scale of song in this method.
    Sometimes the above method be will difficult to find scale because sometimes the last letter will there in the fifth note of the scale.So that cases follow the below instructions.
    • Firstly we have listen the song which you have to play your own until that song comes to your mind.
    • Now we see the last of word in the pallavi and sing that last letter of pallavi continuously.
    • After we have to find that last letter in the keyboard with by singing.Compulsory one note in keyboard was matched with that last letter.
    • Now move five half steps front and play and note.After that we have play two chords(major chord and minor chords).
    • Finally observe which chord was matching.If it is minor chord it is minor scale or it is major chord it was major scale.
    • So you can find the scale of song in this method.
  • Method-2

    • In this method you have find the song's starting letters on the keyboard. BY finding one by one note,you can find scale.It is some what difficult from method1 and consumes more time. You can find the song by seeing below video.
