Practicing chords
While learning keyboard practice is very important.Without practice learning keyboard is very difficult to became a pro.
How to practice these chords?

To play chords in keyboard fast and easy we have to pratice chords in three methods.In these we have to use the tempo,a keyboard and your consenstration.

Three Methods To Practise Chords
Method-1

Firstly set metroname to 4/4 and tempo to 75.Now play a chord with both hands per 4 beats.In 4/4 metroname there are a long beat and three short beats are listen.In this 4 beats play a chords only repeatedly until your hands became free.And repeat the same method but increase the tempo to 150.

You can understand the above method by observing below video.

Method-2

As like above method-1 set metroname to 4/4 and tempo to 75.Now play two chords per 4 beats.In 4/4 metroname there are a long beat and three short beats are listen.In these 4 beats play two chords repeatedly until your hands become more free.And repeat the same method-2 but increase the tempo to 150.

You can understand the above method by observing below video

Method-3

As like above methods set metroname to 4/4 and tempo to 75. Now play four chords per 4 beats.In 4/4 metroname there are a long beat and three short beats are listen.In these 4 beats play four chords repeatedly until your hands become more free.And repeat the same method-3 but increase the tempo to 150.

You can understand the above method by observing below video

Practice the chords this like and make your hand free.As begginers have to pratice this like to play a chords.
